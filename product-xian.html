Xi'an was the capital of 13 different Chinese dynasties and thus has a long and storied history to tell. Xi'an is best known for the life size Terra Cotta Soldiers and the Ancient City Wall surrounding the city.
From Xi'an a train to Bejing (5 hours) or a train to the Shaolin Temple in Zhengzhou City (3 hours) can easily be arranged by our guides.
Another guide will meet you and your family at the train station and ensure you are cared for, from transportation, hotels, traslation and negotiation.
